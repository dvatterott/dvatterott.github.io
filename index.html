I am a postdoctoral research fellow in Jacqueline Gottlieb's lab at Columbia University where I investigate the neural mechanisms underlying visual attention.

I received my PhD from the University of Iowa under Shaun Vecera where I investigated how we learn to direct our attention towards important items and away from unimportant items.
